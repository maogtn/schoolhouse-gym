Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Schoolhouse Gym (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Who We Are

Schoolhouse Gym is located at Unit 8, 58 Cullybackey Link Rd, Ahoghill, Ballymena BT42 1LA. You can contact us at info@schoolhousegym.co.uk or by calling 028 2556 7841.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and business name when you contact us via our website or enquiry form.
  • Membership data — name, contact details, and payment information collected through our ClubLink membership management system.
  • Usage data — anonymous information about how you use our website, collected via Google Analytics (see section 4).
  • Communications — any messages or emails you send us.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide information about our services.
  • Manage your gym membership through ClubLink.
  • Send you information about classes, offers, and gym updates (with your consent).
  • Improve our website and services using anonymised analytics.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This service uses cookies to collect anonymised data such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and how you arrived. This data is not personally identifiable and cannot be used to identify you as an individual.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by declining cookies when prompted.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR:

  • Contract — to manage your membership and deliver our services.
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our services and communicate with existing members.
  • Consent — for marketing communications and analytics cookies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • ClubLink — our membership management platform, which processes membership and payment data on our behalf.
  • Google — for website analytics.
  • Legal authorities — where required by law.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes it was collected for, or as required by law. Membership data is retained for 6 years after your membership ends for accounting and legal purposes.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (the “right to be forgotten”).
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@schoolhousegym.co.uk.

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (session storage for modal preferences).
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics, only set with your consent.

You can manage cookie preferences using the consent banner displayed on your first visit, or through your browser settings.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Payment data is handled exclusively by ClubLink and is not stored on our systems.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

12. Contact & Complaints

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at info@schoolhousegym.co.uk.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO): ico.org.uk • 0303 123 1113.
